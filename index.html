You're Vulnerable

When you go on the internet, your information is at risk.

In 2017 alone, there were 1,579 data breaches resulting in 179 million exposed records. Approximately 1 in 15 people were victims of identity theft.

We hand out personally identifying information - like our email addresses and phone numbers - without any regard for how they expose us.

At best, it means more email and phone spam. But the worst case? Malicious agents can use them to gain access to your identity.

Protecting yourself and those you care about is a matter of reducing risk - ensuring that even if there's one data breach, it doesn't expose you completely.

What Narya Offers

With Narya you can generate phone numbers and email addresses on the fly so you'll never have to give out your personal information again.

This makes it harder for companies and advertisers to track you online, and reduces the risk to you from data breaches if that information is stolen.

Here's how it works:

  1. Register for free on our website.
  2. Generate new emails and phone numbers at the click of a button, and manage where you give out that information.
  3. You'll be able to see what messages go to your custom emails or phone numbers and respond using them - but others won't have access to your real, personal emails or phone numbers anymore.
